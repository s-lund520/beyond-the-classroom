Employment

Finding meaningful work is an important part of building independence and confidence. These resources offer job placement, on-the-job training, and long-term vocational support for students and young adults in Vermont.

HireAbility Vermont

Vermont's official vocational rehabilitation program — where ability meets opportunity. Offers employment services for youth and adults, including job placement, on-the-job support, and resources for parents, schools, and community partners. A great first stop for students transitioning to work.
